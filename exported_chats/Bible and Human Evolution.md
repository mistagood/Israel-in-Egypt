3. **Lead Poisoning in Baltimore (1990s)**: In Baltimore during the 1990s, landlords partnered with the Kennedy Krieger Institute to study lead abatement methods. 5. **Prison Medical Experiments**: Throughout the 20th century, Black inmates were often used as subjects in various medical experiments without their informed consent. These experiments ranged from testing antimalarial drugs to cosmetic products.
