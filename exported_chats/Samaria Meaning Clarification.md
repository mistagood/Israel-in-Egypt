No, Samaria does not mean "of Mary." The name "Samaria" comes from the Hebrew word *Shomron* (שֹׁמְרוֹן), which is derived from the root word *shamar*, meaning "to watch" or "to guard." The name "Mary" (from the Hebrew *Miryam*) has a different origin, likely meaning "bitterness" or "rebelliousness." There is no linguistic connection between Samaria and Mary.
