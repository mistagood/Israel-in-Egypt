How long is a moon cycle 

---



---

A full moon cycle, also known as a lunar cycle or lunation, lasts approximately 29.5 days. This is the time it takes for the moon to go through all its phases, from new moon to full moon and back to new moon.

---

Is there a more precise number?

---

Yes, the more precise length of a lunar cycle, or synodic month, is about **29.530588 days**. This figure accounts for the time it takes the Moon to return to the same phase (e.g., new moon to new moon) as viewed from Earth.
