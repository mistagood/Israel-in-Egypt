Mathematical thinking activates a distinct network of regions in the brain, primarily centered around the **parietal lobe** and **prefrontal cortex**. Here's a breakdown of the key areas:

---

### üß† Brain Regions Involved in Math Processing

#### 1. **Intraparietal Sulcus (IPS)**
- **Location**: Bilateral parietal lobe (more dominant in the left hemisphere for symbolic math).
- **Function**: Core area for numerical magnitude processing‚Äîunderstanding "how many" or "how much".
- **Activation**: Used for estimation, arithmetic, and symbolic reasoning (like algebra).

#### 2. **Angular Gyrus**
- **Location**: Posterior part of the inferior parietal lobe.
- **Function**: Involved in retrieval of arithmetic facts (like multiplication tables), verbal number processing, and linking numbers to language.

#### 3. **Prefrontal Cortex**
- **Location**: Frontal lobe (especially dorsolateral and ventrolateral regions).
- **Function**: Handles working memory, problem-solving, decision-making‚Äîessential for multi-step math operations.
- **Note**: Stronger activation during complex or novel problems.

#### 4. **Posterior Superior Parietal Lobule (PSPL)**
- **Function**: Important for spatial attention and manipulation, especially in geometry and mental rotation tasks.

#### 5. **Fusiform Gyrus (Number Form Area)**
- **Function**: Visual recognition of numbers and symbols; activates when viewing numerals.

---

### üß† Right vs. Left Hemisphere Differences
- **Left Hemisphere**: More active in exact calculations and symbolic math (e.g., 3 + 5).
- **Right Hemisphere**: More involved in approximate calculations and spatial reasoning.

---

### Summary (by math type):
| Math Skill | Brain Area | Function |
|------------|------------|----------|
| Arithmetic | Angular Gyrus & IPS | Retrieval & magnitude comparison |
| Algebraic reasoning | Prefrontal Cortex | Logical structure & working memory |
| Spatial math (geometry) | PSPL & Right Parietal Lobe | Visualization and mental manipulation |
| Approximate estimation | Right IPS | Non-verbal numeric intuition |
