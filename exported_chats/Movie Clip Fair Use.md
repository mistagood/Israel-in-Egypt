Fair use allows limited use of copyrighted material **without permission**, for purposes like:
- Commentary
- Criticism
- News reporting
- Education
- Parody

But fair use is **not guaranteed**, even if you only use a small clip.

#### The Four Fair Use Factors:
1. **Purpose and character** of your use (transformative? non-commercial?)
2. **Nature** of the copyrighted work (factual? creative?)
3. **Amount and substantiality** (how much you used and what part)
4. **Effect** on the market value of the original work
