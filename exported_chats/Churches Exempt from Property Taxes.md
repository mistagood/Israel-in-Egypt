1. **Distinct Legal Existence:** The church must be a distinct legal entity.
2. **Recognized Creed and Form of Worship:** The church must have a recognized creed and form of worship.
3. **Definite and Distinct Ecclesiastical Government:** There must be a clear organizational structure.
4. **Formal Code of Doctrine and Discipline:** The church should have a formal code of doctrine and discipline.
5. **Distinct Religious History:** The church should have a history of being an established religious community.
6. **Membership Not Associated with Other Churches:** The membership should be distinct from other church organizations.
7. **Ordained Ministers:** The church must have ordained ministers who are recognized as church leaders.
8. **Literature of its Own:** The church should have its own religious literature.
9. **Established Places of Worship:** The church must have established places of worship.
10. **Regular Congregations:** The church should have a regular congregation.
11. **Regular Religious Services:** The church must hold regular religious services.
12. **Sunday Schools for Religious Instruction:** If applicable, the church should provide Sunday schools for religious instruction of the young.
13. **Schools for Preparation of its Ministers:** If applicable, the church should have schools for the preparation of its ministers.

### Where to Demonstrate Compliance

1. **Form 1023, Application for Recognition of Exemption Under Section 501(c)(3):**
   - When applying for tax-exempt status, you will need to complete Form 1023 or Form 1023-EZ and provide detailed information about your organization.

2. **Attachments to Form 1023:**
   - Provide copies of your Articles of Organization, bylaws, and other governing documents.

3. **Narrative Description of Activities:**
   - Provide a comprehensive narrative that describes your religious activities, governance structure, religious history, and other relevant information.

4. **Supporting Documentation:**

### Relevant IRS Publications and Forms

- **Form 1023 and Form 1023 Instructions:** Detailed instructions for completing the application for recognition of exemption.

You can find the forms and publications on the [IRS website](https://www.irs.gov/). Specifically, Publication 1828 is very helpful in understanding the IRS’s requirements for churches.
