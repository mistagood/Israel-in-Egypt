# Tree of knowledge and demise



---



---

The tree of knowledge leading to death equals the over-specialization of humanity in ingenuity and technology, knowledge production, etc., our oversized brains. Our specialization, our niche, we become over-specialized and that leads to our demise because we become dependent on the things that we have invented to survive. And so all of civilization has gone down that route. Now without those things, civilization is in peril, or would be in peril.
