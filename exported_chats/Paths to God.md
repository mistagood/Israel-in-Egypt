# Paths to God



---

Pope Francis recently stated that all religions are a road to God or something to that effect. Can you check that quote? I'd like to go deeper into it because I had my own revelation on this in the past week as well. So maybe this is something that is a message that is time has been given to humanity to pay attention to at this moment. So what we find is that we'll see the same themes over and over in religion, the same stories being told. The characters and their names may change some of the events and orders, but we see the same occurrences, virgin births and so forth. These are because there's one story that's being told. And if we get down to the core of that story and strip away the cultural additions to it, then we will find the truth of it. This has to be so if we want all of the cultures of the world to be able to experience God. How could they experience the same God if they didn't have a different name for him? They wouldn't be able to personally know him. You wouldn't be able to personally understand or relate God to their lives, their environment, their experiences, their history, their ancestors. And so we see the stories unfold to say the same thing over and over again. And so it is true that at the core we can find God through any of the paths as long as we go deep enough and find the truth in them.
