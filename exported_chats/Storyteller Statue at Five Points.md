The statue at Five Points South is known as "The Storyteller" or "The Frank Fleming Fountain." It's a prominent sculpture located in Birmingham, Alabama, created by artist Frank Fleming. The centerpiece of the fountain is a ram-man figure, surrounded by various smaller animals, all listening intently to the ram-man's story. This whimsical sculpture is not just a local landmark but also a symbol of community and storytelling, reflecting the cultural and artistic vibrancy of the Five Points South neighborhood.
