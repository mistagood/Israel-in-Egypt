It marked the third and final major Jewish–Roman conflict, following the First Jewish–Roman War and the Kitos War. Initially, Emperor Hadrian showed some favor towards the Jews, permitting them to return to Jerusalem and even granting permission for the rebuilding of their Holy Temple.
