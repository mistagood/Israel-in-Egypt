## 1. Abraham’s Entry and the First Land-Promise

When Abram first enters Canaan, the narrative immediately anchors the divine land-promise at Shechem. After leaving Laban, Jacob returns to Canaan and comes specifically to Shechem. Genesis 33:18–19 states that Jacob “bought a parcel of a field” from the sons of Hamor for one hundred pieces of money. Genesis 34 describes the intended union between Dinah and Shechem, the prince of the land.
