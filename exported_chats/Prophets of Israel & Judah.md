In the Bible, prophets were divided between those who prophesied in the northern kingdom of Israel and those in the southern kingdom of Judah. Here is a list of notable prophets, the periods they prophesied, and the kingdoms they addressed:

### Prophets of the Northern Kingdom (Israel)

1. **Amos**
   - **Period:** 8th century BCE (circa 760-750 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Against Israel's social injustices, idolatry, and complacency.
   - **Kingdom:** Northern Kingdom of Israel

2. **Hosea**
   - **Period:** 8th century BCE (circa 755-710 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Focused on Israel's unfaithfulness and the need for repentance.
   - **Kingdom:** Northern Kingdom of Israel

3. **Elijah**
   - **Period:** 9th century BCE (circa 870-850 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Condemned the worship of Baal and supported the worship of Yahweh.
   - **Kingdom:** Northern Kingdom of Israel

4. **Elisha**
   - **Period:** 9th century BCE (circa 850-800 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Continued Elijah's work, performing miracles and advising the kings.
   - **Kingdom:** Northern Kingdom of Israel

### Prophets of the Southern Kingdom (Judah)

1. **Isaiah**
   - **Period:** 8th century BCE (circa 740-681 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Spoke about Judah's need for repentance and the coming of the Messiah.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

2. **Jeremiah**
   - **Period:** Late 7th century to early 6th century BCE (circa 626-586 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Warned of Judah's destruction and exile due to their sins.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

3. **Micah**
   - **Period:** 8th century BCE (circa 740-700 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Denounced the corruption of Judah and foretold the fall of Jerusalem.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

4. **Habakkuk**
   - **Period:** Late 7th century BCE (circa 620-600 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Questioned God about the suffering of the righteous and the prosperity of the wicked.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

5. **Zephaniah**
   - **Period:** 7th century BCE (circa 640-609 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Warned of the coming Day of the Lord and the need for Judah's repentance.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

6. **Joel**
   - **Period:** Uncertain, possibly 9th or 5th century BCE
   - **Prophecy:** Called for repentance and spoke of the coming Day of the Lord.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

7. **Nahum**
   - **Period:** 7th century BCE (circa 660-630 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Pronounced the downfall of Nineveh and Assyria.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

8. **Obadiah**
   - **Period:** 6th century BCE (after the fall of Jerusalem in 586 BCE)
   - **Prophecy:** Condemned Edom for their role in the fall of Jerusalem.
   - **Kingdom:** Southern Kingdom of Judah

This list includes some of the major prophets and their primary focus, providing a glimpse into their significant roles in the history of Israel and Judah.
