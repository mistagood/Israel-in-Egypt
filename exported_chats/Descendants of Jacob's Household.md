## TRIBAL ALLOTMENT OF JOSEPH'S SONS: A TEXTUAL SURVEY

- **Joshua 14:4**: _“For the children of Joseph were two tribes, Manasseh and Ephraim: therefore they gave no part unto the Levites in the land, save cities to dwell in...”_

This distinction sets up a **double inheritance** for Joseph through his sons.

### 2. **EPHRAIM'S ALLOTMENT**
**Primary Source: Joshua 16:1–10**

- The territory begins at the **Jordan east of Jericho**.
- Passes westward through **Bethel**, **Ataroth**, and toward **Gezer**, ending at the **Mediterranean Sea**.
- **Verse 9**: _“And the separate cities for the children of Ephraim were among the inheritance of the children of Manasseh...”_  
  → Ephraim held **cities embedded within Manasseh’s territory**.

**Cities included:**
- Bethel (border town near Benjamin)
- Ataroth
- Naarah
- Gezer (Ephraim didn’t drive out the Canaanites—see Joshua 16:10)

### 6. **POSSIBLE ENCLAVES WITHIN JUDAH?**

There is **no explicit verse** stating that Joseph’s sons were granted **land inside the tribal territory of Judah**.

- **Numbers 36:7** makes it illegal for land to transfer between tribes permanently.

**Conclusion**: The land in **Bethlehem** held by **Ephraimites** must have been:
1. A **Josephite enclave**, or
2. Part of **original inter-tribal allocation not documented in surviving texts**.
